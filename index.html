About Us

Growing up, I had every opportunity to learn and experience everything related to media up close that it had almost become a part of me.At age 17, I tried my hand at doing photography for events. Business quickly grew solely by word of mouth from happy, satisfied clients who liked to work with us.

D Crafts started evolving after I got into event management and started doing works for the industry's leading channels and companies pan India.I gained more knowledge from working in this field from a very young age and from the guidance of experienced mentors than I could ever gain from any school or college.

We have now expanded our spectrum of services to social media marketing, film promotions and a post production hub.
There's still a lot more to come!

Today, D Crafts is run by a group of youngsters, whose job has been tailored to suit each of their unique talents and work style and not vice versa.
Our goal for this company is to always deliver to our clients the best work that we can possibly offer and also to push our boundaries by trying out new creative ideas and innovative technology, so that both sides take back something valuable from the result.
